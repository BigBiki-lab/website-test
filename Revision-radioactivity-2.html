Revision Radioactivity 2

What happens to one or more electrons when an atom gains energy?

jump to higher orbits / shells / energy levels

Name two ways in which an atom can gain energy?

by heating, by electricity, by electromagnetic radiation

What happens to electrons when the atom loses energy?

They fall to a lower orbit / shell / energy level.

What is ionisation?

when an atom loses or gains electrons

What is background radiation?

Answer: Background radiation is low-level ionising radiation that is always present in the environment from various sources.
