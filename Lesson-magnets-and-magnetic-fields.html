Magnets and magnetic fields

Learning objective: What shape are magnetic fields and how can they be plotted?

What are the two ends of a bar magnet called?

north-seeking pole and south-seeking pole, or north and south pole

Name two magnetic materials.

iron, cobalt, nickel, steel

What happens if you arrange two magnets with their north poles close to each other?

They repel each other.

How can you arrange two magnets so they attract each other?

put opposite poles close to each other/put a north pole near a south pole

History of magnets

The ancient Greeks in an area identified as Magnesia described the magnetic property of natural ferric ferrite (Fe3O4) stones (lodestones). During the time, a shepherd identified by some people as Magnes noticed the nails of his shoes stuck on the metal tip of a black rock he was standing on.

Plotting magnetic field lines

You can use plotting compasses to show the direction and shape of a magnetic field.

Method

- Place the ________ compass near the magnet and mark the position of the end of the needle.
- Move the ________ _______ so the tail of the needle is on the previous dot and mark the point of the needle again.
- Repeat to produce a line, then start again near the ______.
- Keep repeating until the direction of the field all around the magnet has been plotted.
