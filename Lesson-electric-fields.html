Electric fields

Learning objective: How do electric fields help to explain static electrical effects?

Which charged particles move when something gains a charge?

electrons

If you walk across a nylon carpet you may pick up a charge. Why does this charge not leave you?

nylon is an insulator

If you have a negative charge, why might you feel a shock when touching a metal door handle?

electrons move (from you to the metal)

What is the process of removing charge from an object called?

earthing
