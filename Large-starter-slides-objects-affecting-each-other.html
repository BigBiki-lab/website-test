Objects affecting each other

1. Which of the following is the SI unit for power?

a) Joule(J)

b) Watt(W)

c) Newton(N)

d) Pascal(Pa)

b) Watt(W)

2. If the work done on an object is zero, but the force applied is not zero, what can be concluded?

a) The object moved in the direction of the force.

b) The displacement is perpendicular to the force.

c) The velocity is increasing.

d) The force is negative.

b) The displacement is perpendicular to the force.

3. A force of 10N acts on an object, moving it 5m in the same direction. How much work is done?

a) 2J

b) 15J

c) 50J

d) 100J

c) 50J

4. If you double the time taken to perform a certain amount of work, the power developed is:

a) Doubled

b) Halved

c) Quadrupled

d) Unchanged

b) Halved
