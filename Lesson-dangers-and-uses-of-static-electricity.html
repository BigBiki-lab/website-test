Dangers and uses of static electricity

Learning objective: Understand the dangers and uses of static electricity.

What type of materials can collect an electric charge: conductors or insulators?

insulators

Which particles move when an object gains a charge?

electrons

What force causes the movement of these particles when an object gains a charge?

friction

A polythene rod is rubbed with a duster. The duster becomes positively charged. What is the charge on the rod now?

negative
