What is power?

The energy transferred by an electric current depends on the time taken, so it is often more useful to compare the power of the appliances.

Power is the energy transferred per second.

This is often shown on appliances as the power rating.

Power is measured in watts, (W).

1W is a transfer of 1 joule per second.

Calculate the energy transferred by a 800 W microwave in 1 minute.

Answer: E = 48 000 J

A kettle transfers 540 000 J of energy in 3 minutes. Calculate the power of the kettle.

Answer: P = 3 000 W
